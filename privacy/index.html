Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how MiroFish handles information when people visit the website, evaluate the product, contact the team, or use hosted MiroFish services.

This policy is written for the public MiroFish website at mirofish.best and related hosted product flows.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, email support@aigeamy.com.

For sales and enterprise deployment questions, email mirofish@shanda.com.

Information we may collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, organization, support messages, sales inquiries, account details, payment-related records, and any scenario data or files you intentionally submit to MiroFish.

We may also collect basic technical and usage information, such as pages viewed, referring pages, approximate device and browser information, timestamps, and product events used to understand reliability, security, and feature usage.

How we use information

  • To operate, maintain, secure, and improve MiroFish.
  • To provide simulations, reports, hosted workspaces, support, and account-related services.
  • To respond to product questions, enterprise inquiries, and support requests.
  • To understand website and product performance through analytics.
  • To prevent abuse, troubleshoot incidents, enforce terms, and comply with legal obligations.

Submitted content and simulations

MiroFish is designed around seed data, scenarios, generated agents, simulation runs, and reports. You should only submit content that you have the right to use. Hosted services may process submitted content to generate simulations, reports, and interactive analysis.

If you self-host MiroFish, your local deployment and chosen infrastructure determine where your data is stored and processed.

Service providers

We may use trusted service providers for hosting, analytics, payments, email, support, security, and infrastructure. These providers are expected to process information only as needed to provide their services to MiroFish.

Data retention and security

We keep information for as long as needed to operate the service, meet legal or accounting requirements, resolve disputes, and improve reliability. We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, but no online service can guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, deletion, or export of personal information, subject to applicable laws and legitimate operational needs. You can also avoid submitting sensitive or confidential content unless your deployment and agreement are designed for that use.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as MiroFish evolves. When changes are material, we will update the date on this page and provide additional notice where appropriate.
