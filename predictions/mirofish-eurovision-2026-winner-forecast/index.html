MiroFish forecast question

Who will win Eurovision 2026?

The useful MiroFish angle is not "what is Eurovision?" The useful angle is how competing signals change in the final week: market conviction, fan enthusiasm, jury compatibility, staging notes, and the possibility of a late televote surprise.

What would change the forecast

  • A finalist underperforms in jury-show or rehearsal reporting.
  • Market probability compresses sharply around Denmark or Greece.
  • Televote discussion shifts toward a high-energy outsider.
  • Official running order or staging narratives create a late visibility advantage.
