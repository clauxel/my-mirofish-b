MiroFish forecast question

Who will win the 2026 FIFA World Cup?

This page is designed to update over time. Early tournament forecasts should not pretend certainty; MiroFish uses them to track how draw path, health, and group-stage performance change the probability landscape.

What would change the forecast

  • Confirmed group path and knockout bracket difficulty after group-stage results.
  • Major injury or selection news for a top contender.
  • Market odds shifting from a two-team lead to a broader field.
  • Travel, climate, or scheduling constraints that affect recovery.
