Which team will win the 2026 NBA Finals?

The MiroFish view is focused on how probabilities should update as playoff paths narrow. A title market can move from team quality to matchup specificity very quickly.

What would change the forecast

  • A confirmed injury to a top usage player or defensive anchor.
  • A series lead shift that changes rest and matchup expectations.
  • Evidence that a lower-probability team has a favorable Finals matchup.
  • A market move that compresses OKC below a simple majority.
